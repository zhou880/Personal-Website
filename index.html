About

Hey everyone!

My name is Tim Zhou and I am originally from New Jersey. However, you'll currently find me in Indiana at Purdue University. I am a Junior majoring in Computer Engineering and minoring in Business Ecnomics in the Honors College.



I am extremely passionate about making a difference in the world with technology. I am a driven individual who loves working with others to come up with challenging solutions. Some of my interests include full stack development, cloud computing, data engineering, automation, and AI. At school you might find me giving out engineering tours, working on a startup, FroYo XPress, helping students in the lab, hanging out with friends, and of course, studying in the library.



Last summer I was a Software Engineering Intern at John Deere where I worked with data to create a diagnostic mapping tool. This summer, I was an AI & Automation Intern at Siemens Financial Services, where I worked on a full stack application that helped streamline the credit approval process.



Don't hesitate to contact me! I'd love to connect with anyone who wants to have a conversation.
